About Me

Passionate about creating accessible, performant, and beautiful web experiences.

My Story

I'm Alex Chen, a full-stack developer and UI designer based in San Francisco. With over 8 years of experience in web development, I've had the privilege of working with startups, agencies, and enterprise companies to build digital products that make a difference.

My journey into web development started when I was 15, building fan sites for my favorite video games. What began as a hobby quickly turned into a passion. I realized that the web had the power to connect people, share knowledge, and create meaningful experiences.

Today, I specialize in building accessible web applications using modern technologies like React, Next.js, and Node.js. But what truly sets my work apart is my commitment to inclusive design. I believe the web should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their abilities or the devices they use.

My Approach

Every project I work on starts with accessibility in mind. From semantic HTML structure to keyboard navigation, from color contrast to screen reader support — these aren't afterthoughts but foundational elements of my development process.

I also believe in the power of simplicity. Clean code, clear design, and straightforward user experiences are at the heart of what I create. Performance isn't just about fast load times; it's about respecting your users' time and attention.

Beyond Code

When I'm not coding, you can find me contributing to open-source accessibility tools, mentoring aspiring developers, or exploring the hiking trails around the Bay Area. I'm also an avid reader and enjoy books on design, psychology, and technology ethics.
