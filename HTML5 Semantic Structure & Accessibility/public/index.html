About Me

With over 8 years of experience in web development, I specialize in creating accessible, performant, and visually appealing digital experiences. My approach combines technical expertise with a deep commitment to inclusive design.

I believe the web should be accessible to everyone, regardless of ability. That's why every project I work on prioritizes WCAG compliance, semantic HTML, and progressive enhancement.
