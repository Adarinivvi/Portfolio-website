Semantic HTML is the foundation of accessible web development. By using the right elements for the right purpose, we create documents that are meaningful to both humans and machines.

This article explores why semantic HTML matters and how to use it effectively:

Key Concepts:

  • Understanding document outline and heading hierarchy
  • Using landmark elements (header, nav, main, footer)
  • Choosing the right element: article vs section vs div
  • Benefits for screen readers and assistive technology
  • SEO advantages of semantic markup
